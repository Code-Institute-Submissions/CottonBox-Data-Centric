What are cookies?

Cookies are a small text file of data that is stored on your computer by the web browser while you are browsing a website.

Cookies are designed to be a reliabel mechanism for websites to remember important information.

How we use cookies?

Here at Cotton Box, we use cookies to help provide a better user experience when browsing our site.

What cookies do we store?

These are the cookies that we create and store:

  • cottonbox_accept: This is created when you click the accept button on the cookie consent form when you load the home page.
  • callout_closed: This remembers whether or not you have closed the callout on the home page so it does not reopen when you visit it again.

    • How to manage cookies?

    Each browser has their own location where users are able to manage cookies.

    On chrome for example:

    1. Click on the lock icon to the left of the address bar.
    2. Click on the cookies option.
    3. There you will be able to see all of the cookies that have been created and stored when you visited the website.
      Here you are able to delete any cookies that you no longer wish to have stored on your computer.

    For other browsers, search for how to disable/remove cookies on your particular browser.
